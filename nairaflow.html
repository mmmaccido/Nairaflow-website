Flexible delivery

Four ways to deliver

Every family situation is different. We give you the delivery option that makes sense for your recipient's location and needs.

Our unique advantage

Hand-to-hand cash delivery

For recipients in Nigeria who prefer or need physical naira cash. Our trusted agent network delivers directly to their door or a nearby pickup point.

  • Physical cash in naira
  • Door-to-door delivery available
  • 4-hour delivery SLA in major cities
  • Agent confirmed pickup with code
  • No bank account required

Nigerian bank transfer

Direct transfer to any Nigerian bank account. Supports all commercial and microfinance banks via NIP/NIBSS instant payment rails.

  • All Nigerian banks supported
  • NIP instant payment
  • Usually under 5 minutes
  • SMS confirmation to recipient

International bank wire

Send directly to the student's foreign bank account in their local currency. No conversion required on their end.

  • USD, CAD, GBP, EUR, AUD and more
  • Powered by Wise API
  • ACH, SEPA, Faster Payments, Interac
  • Full bank details required

Virtual USD / foreign wallet

Fund the student's virtual foreign currency account on platforms like Grey or Geegpay — popular with Nigerian students abroad.

  • Instant wallet credit
  • Works with Grey, Geegpay
  • Student can spend with card
  • Ideal for recurring allowances

Frequently asked questions

Transfer times vary by destination and delivery method. Nigerian bank transfers complete in under 5 minutes. International wires via Wise take minutes to 1–2 business days depending on the destination country. Cash hand delivery has a 4-hour SLA in major Nigerian cities.
For amounts under ₦500,000, you only need a valid phone number and BVN verification. For larger amounts, we may request a government-issued ID (NIN, International Passport, or Driver's Licence) as required by CBN guidelines. This is a one-time process.
We use live interbank exchange rates updated every 5 minutes and add a small transparent margin (typically 1–1.5%). The exact rate you see in the calculator is the rate you get — it is locked in for 60 minutes once you initiate a transfer. For volatile currencies like Turkish Lira, the lock window is 30 minutes.
Once your naira payment is confirmed, we assign a verified local agent near the recipient's address. The agent contacts the recipient, delivers the cash, and the recipient confirms with a one-time code sent to their phone. You receive an SMS when delivery is complete. Currently available in Lagos, Abuja, Kano, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, and Bida.
Minimum transfer is ₦5,000. Daily limit is ₦5,000,000 for verified accounts. Monthly limit is ₦20,000,000. Higher limits are available for business accounts — contact us for details.
